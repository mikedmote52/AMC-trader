# VIGL STRATEGY EXIT PROTOCOL
## When to Hold vs When to Fold

---

## üéØ THE PROBLEM

Holding for +100% is great... until the stock drops -40% and you give back all gains.

**Need:** Early warning system to exit BEFORE major damage.

---

## üö® EXIT TRIGGERS (In Priority Order)

### 1. HARD STOP (Never Violate)
**-15% from entry price**
- Cut immediately, no exceptions
- Preserves capital for next trade
- **Applies to:** All positions, regardless of thesis

### 2. TRAILING STOP (Protects Gains)
**-15% from highest price reached**
- Example: Buy at $10, runs to $20, stop at $17 (15% below $20)
- Lets winners run, protects profits
- **Applies to:** All positions in profit

### 3. MOMENTUM BREAKDOWN (Early Warning)
**Technical signals:**
- Closes below 20-day EMA for 2+ days
- Volume dries up on rallies (no buying interest)
- Lower highs + lower lows pattern
- Breaks key support level

**Action:** Scale out 50% immediately, trail remaining 50% tightly (-10%)

### 4. TIME STOP (Opportunity Cost)
**Position flat for 10+ trading days**
- No momentum = dead money
- Exit and redeploy capital
- **Exception:** If still above entry, hold with tight trail

### 5. PROFIT PROTECTION (Already Up Big)
**Position up +50%+, then drops -20% from peak**
- Example: Runs to $15 (+50%), drops to $12 (-20% from peak)
- Scale out 50% immediately
- Trail remaining 50% at -10%
