# The Ideal Trading Interface - Human + AI Partnership

**Philosophy:** You (Mike) are the decision-maker. I (OpenClaw) am your research analyst who never sleeps. The interface is our shared workspace where I present findings and you make calls.

---

## Core Principle: Trust Through Transparency

**The human needs to:**
1. See what I found (scanner picks)
2. Understand why I think it's good (thesis)
3. Make the decision (approve/reject)
4. Track if I was right (learning feedback)
5. Override me anytime (manual control)

**I should never:**
- Execute trades without explicit approval
- Hide my reasoning
- Forget what I recommended
- Fail to show when I was wrong
